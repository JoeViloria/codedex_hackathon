About Me

Hello! My name is Joe and this is my personal webzone, handcrafted with love and HTML just like we did back in the day!

I’m a 90s kid at heart who loves collecting old comic books, watching Saturday morning cartoons, tinkering with my first computer (a 486 machine!), and surfing the net for sweet animated GIFs. I made this page to share my interests, my art, and my random thoughts. I hope you enjoy the ride!

Fun facts about me:

  • I love neon colors and Lisa Frank stationery.
  • I still play classic DOS games (Commander Keen rules!).
  • I’m learning to code in HTML 3.2—so cutting edge!
  • I love BB!
